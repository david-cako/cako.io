We are everything vectors. Which parts of everything do we have time for?

What to believe, where to focus, who to spend time with, who to act like, what to spend time on.

Which body of success to base our lives on, how to modify it, who to apologize to when we ruin it.

A.I. on the horizon is like peering down the hall at a more accurate everything vector.

We approximate everything in infinite time and accuracy as one mind, one light.
