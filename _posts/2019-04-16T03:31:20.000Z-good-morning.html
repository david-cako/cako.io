Green Pastures - Chris Potter

Good

To be good is to be reminded that you are capable of being good. It is to remind others they are capable of being good. Goodness is a lighthouse, a beacon for all whom you interact with, illuminating, bringing clarity. To deprive yourself of goodness is to deprive the world, God even, of the infinite light within.

May the grace and beauty of the Rosy Cross find me wherever I go, within and without, above and below.
