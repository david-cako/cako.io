--- title: "Stumblin across Logic" date: "2019-08-01T07:02:09.000Z" --- # Stumblin across Logic 2019-08-01T07:02:09.000Z

I spent the past week obsessively learning Logic Pro.

To me, the feeling of pulling back the curtain on magic is unlike any other; to go from observer to participant. I felt it this strongly just a few times before, notably writing software. You only get so many of these in life I think.

Beats are floating around in my head. Knowing the next question to search seems like the only true limitation in the 21st century. I think that is incredible.


Humble beginnings. Each one I make is a little better.
