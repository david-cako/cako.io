Nujabes - Peaceland

There are so many things I can take responsibility for, big and small, good and bad -- I am behind this body and consciousness, after all.

This inspired reality we share is a push and pull of so many individual wills; consciousness that we share on some level, and that we are responsible for nurturing as far as we can reach.

So many people have done so many great and not-so-great things for me, and I have done many great and not-so-great things for others.

The only true and rational choice is to let go of what is lost, love all that is, and shed light on all to come. A life lived anxious and resentful is a waste. A life lived purely, thoughtfully, and immediately is great.

Suddenly we are one, here at last to love and breathe.
