--- title: "The Magician" date: "2019-08-27T04:34:00.000Z" --- # The Magician 2019-08-27T04:34:00.000Z

Akin to a lightning rod, the Magician is an emissary of a limitless spark. The Magician's power of attention and understanding produces new heights of beauty and alchemical output, at once unifying his/her conscious state with the universal state of will-to-good.

The Magician enhances the prima materia, the universal Self, through spontaneous and accurate purification.

Tools assembled, the Magician is made susceptible to his point of concentration. Calling upon experience and fixing his attention, he cultivates and adorns the always-purified state of this Great Work.
