--- title: "Grateful" date: "2019-09-18T05:07:21.000Z" --- # Grateful 2019-09-18T05:07:21.000Z

Sufjan Stevens - The Transfiguration

I am grateful for my close, caring family that shows me laughter and joy; for all of the loving, insightful souls that have taught me meaning and purpose.
For the experiences that have shown me truth is not certain.
For my great coworkers that always want to help.
For the food, shelter, and luxury that I assume.
For the acuity that affords me body and life.

I am forever grateful for this incredible world we share, painting with our thoughts and feelings.
