--- title: "Pneumatic" date: "2018-12-18T15:11:09.000Z" --- # Pneumatic 2018-12-18T15:11:09.000Z

Pneumatic: fully spiritual, acquainted with perfect knowledge, unafflicted, unrestrained.

He orients forward with the wind, he is carried effortlessly towards the horizon, he breathes to the rhythm of the universe.

That which carries is that which is carried; equal aspects of the same thing.
