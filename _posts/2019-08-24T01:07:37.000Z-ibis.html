--- title: "Ibis" date: "2019-08-24T01:07:37.000Z" --- # Ibis 2019-08-24T01:07:37.000Z


Bless this freedom. Bless this holy immunity from non-truth.

Bless this power of experience and reason to overwhelm all else.

The solution we seek lies within this half-full glass of empty.

As I tip this glass over, an outpouring of fullness envelops me and drains downstream.

The solution that we seek is cast out across the universe.

What I understand of you I may never know, but in knowing I find I may never understand. Clarity and truth in this affirmation is the most noble goal.
